An absolute must-do experience at Tamasaki Shrine is walking the **'Barefoot Path'** that circles the main sanctuary.
It is a ritual where you take off your shoes and socks and walk **clockwise three times** on a path laid with smooth, white pebbles (sacred jade pebbles).

- **1st lap**: You'll feel a tingling pain in the soles of your feet. (Legend has it, the worse your health, the more it hurts!)
- **2nd lap**: Amidst the pain, you begin to feel the cool energy of the earth and the acupressure effect.
- **3rd lap**: The pain soon turns into a refreshing coolness, and you can feel the negative energy in your body being released through the soles of your feet.

This path, with its legend that "walking barefoot grants your wish," is Tamasaki Shrine's most powerful purification (detox) spot. The warm, tingling sensation in your feet after the walk is proof that you have properly received the earth's energy.
