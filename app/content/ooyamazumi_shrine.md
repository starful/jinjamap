### 2. ğŸŒ² Strolling the Precincts: A Millennial Camphor Tree and a Wish-Granting Gate

The precincts are filled with a sacred energy emanating from a vast forest of camphor trees.

#### **2,600-Year-Old Sacred Tree (Ochi-no-Miko's Camphor Tree)**

Upon entering the precincts, an overwhelmingly large camphor tree captures your attention. With a circumference of 11 meters, this tree is said to have been planted by the shrine's founder and is an astonishing **2,600 years old**. Standing before the tree and taking a deep breath feels like receiving primeval energy throughout your body. (Designated as a Natural Monument)

#### **Sacred Tree of Life's Water (Ikiki-no-Gomon)**

On the forest path behind the main hall, there is the **'Sacred Tree of Life's Water,'** where two camphor trees with connected roots have naturally formed a gate. Legend has it that passing through this tree tunnel grants **long life and health** and makes wishes come true. Feel reborn as you pass through the narrow gap between the trees.

#### **Main Hall and Worship Hall**

Rebuilt in 1427, the main hall is a wooden structure in the unique **'Mishima-zukuri'** style. Its unadorned, simple yet solid appearance exudes a dignity befitting a shrine dedicated to the god of war.

### 4. ğŸš² Nearby Attractions: Bicycles and Sea Bream Cuisine

After visiting the shrine, be sure to enjoy the charms of Omishima.

#### **1. Treasure Museum (Shihokan)**

- **Location**: Right next to the shrine precincts.
- **Features**: **Absolutely do not miss this!** 80% of Japan's National Treasures and Important Cultural Properties in the form of arms and armor are here. This is your chance to see treasures you've only read about in textbooks, like Yoshitsune's armor and Japanese swords. The admission fee is well worth it.

#### **2. Shimanami Kaido Cycling**

- **Features**: A world-renowned cycling course that runs over the sea. Rent a bicycle near the shrine and try crossing the Tatara Bridge. The exhilarating feeling of riding with the sea breeze will become an unforgettable memory.

#### **3. 'Tairyo,' a Taimeshi (Sea Bream Rice Bowl) Restaurant**

- **Location**: In front of the main gate of the shrine.
- **Menu**: A specialty restaurant for **sea bream**, a famous product of Ehime Prefecture. The rice bowl piled high with fresh sea bream sashimi is top-notch in both value and taste. (Be aware of long lines during lunchtime!)
