The main deity (ä¸»ç¥­ç¥) here is **Wakahirume-no-Mikoto**. Her name means 'young and vibrant sun goddess,' and she is the younger sister of the sun goddess Amaterasu and a master of weaving.
As a deity who creates fabric by weaving threads, she demonstrates exceptional ability in forging **'connections (enmusubi)'** between people. Visitors pray not only for romantic relationships but also for good fortune in all human connections, including friendships and professional opportunities.

#### **ã€Origins and History: Rising Again Like a Phoenixã€‘**

The shrine's history dates back to 201 AD. When Empress Jingu's fleet was unable to proceed off the coast of Kobe, she received a divine message and built a shrine at this location.
Ikuta Shrine has faced numerous trials. It was a fierce battlefield during the Genpei War in the 12th century, suffered through air raids in World War II, and endured the Great Hanshin Earthquake in 1995. However, each time, it was magnificently rebuilt through the efforts of its citizens. For this reason, it is known as a powerful spot for **'rebirth'** and **'regeneration.'** It is a place that offers great courage to those who wish to overcome failure and start anew.

### 2. ğŸŒ² A Stroll Through the Grounds: A Mystical Forest and Water Fortune in the City

Just a 10-minute walk from the bustling Sannomiya Station. Pass through the red torii gate, and a completely different world unfolds.

#### **Main Hall and Ikuta Forest**

Behind the magnificent vermilion main hall lies the **'Ikuta Forest.'** This dense forest, unbelievable for its location in the heart of the city, is as beautiful as it was when praised by poets a thousand years ago. A walk through the forest, breathing in the fresh air, will clear your cluttered mind.

#### **Ikuta Pond & Mizumikuji (Water Fortune)**

The clear pond within the forest is a highlight of Ikuta Shrine. Be sure to try the famous **'Mizumikuji (water fortune).'** When you float the blank paper slip on the pond's surface, characters magically appear to reveal your fortune. Don't miss this romantic and mystical experience!

#### **The Powerful Life Force of the Sacred Cedar Tree**

Next to the main hall stands a giant cedar tree. This tree, which survived the fires of war, is a symbol of powerful life force. Place your hand on the tree and pray for health and recovery.
