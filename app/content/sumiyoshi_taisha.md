The grounds of Sumiyoshi Taisha are filled with a unique beauty. Here are the key points you shouldn't miss.

#### **Sorihashi Bridge: The Rainbow Bridge to the Realm of the Gods**

Upon entering, the first thing that catches your eye is the red arch bridge, **'Sorihashi.'** Its reflection on the water's surface resembles a taiko (drum), earning it the nickname 'Taikobashi.' With a maximum incline of 48 degrees, it is challenging to climb, but it is said that simply crossing this bridge **"washes away the sins and impurities of the body and mind."** The view from the top of the bridge is absolutely spectacular!

#### **The Sumiyoshi-zukuri Main Sanctuaries, National Treasures**

After crossing the bridge, four main sanctuaries appear. The first, second, and third sanctuaries are arranged in a line, while the fourth is placed beside them. This unique layout is said to represent the formation of a fleet of ships sailing the sea. These buildings, constructed in the **'Sumiyoshi-zukuri'** styleâ€”the oldest style of shrine architecture in Japanâ€”are all designated as **National Treasures**. Experience the essence of ancient architecture created by the harmony of red, white, and straight-lined roofs.

- **Omokaruishi (Heavy-Light Stone)**: Make a wish and lift the stone. If it feels lighter than expected, your wish will come true! If it feels heavy, it's a message from the gods that more effort is needed.

### 4. ğŸš‹ Nearby Attractions: The Romance of the Tram and Retro Osaka

After visiting the shrine, take some time to experience the atmosphere of southern Osaka.

#### **1. Hankai Tramway (Chin-Chin Tram)**

- **Location**: Right in front of the shrine.
- **Features**: This is Osaka's only tram line. The feeling of riding a clattering tram that's over 100 years old is pure romance! Try using it to travel to Tennoji or Abeno Harukas.

#### **2. Abeno Harukas**

- **Access**: 15 minutes by the Hankai Tramway.
- **Features**: Japan's tallest building (300m). Enjoy a panoramic view of Osaka from the observation deck to highlight your trip.

#### **3. Sumiyoshi Park & Local Eateries**

- **Location**: Near the shrine.
- **Features**: Many long-established eateries are hidden here. Be sure to try local foods beloved by residents, such as the Western-style restaurant **'Yoshoku Sueyoshi'** or **'Tamago Korokke'** (egg croquettes).
