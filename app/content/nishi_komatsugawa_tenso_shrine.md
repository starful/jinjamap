### 3. ğŸ“œ Goshuin and Shrine Items: Blessings of the Solar Deity

Visitors can receive a traditional **Goshuin** (red ink stamp) that bears the elegant characters of "Tenso Shrine." Being dedicated to Amaterasu, the shrine also offers various *Omamori* (amulets) focused on **general protection** and **warding off evil** (*Yakuyoke*). The design of the items often reflects the simplicity and purity associated with Shinmei-style architecture and the Sun Goddess.
