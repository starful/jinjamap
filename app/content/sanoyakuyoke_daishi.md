In 944, the legendary warrior **Fujiwara no Hidesato** founded the temple. He was a valiant general famous for the 'Legend of the Centipede Slayer.' After suppressing a rebellion, he invited Gansan Daishi, the most revered high priest of the time, to establish this temple.
For over a thousand years, this place has soothed the sorrows of the common people and averted calamities, making it one of the three great Daishi temples in the Kanto region.

### 2. ğŸ¯ A Stroll Through the Precincts: The Golden Bell and Purification of the Heart

The precincts are not large, but every step is filled with spiritual energy.

#### **The Golden Temple Bell and Main Hall**

Upon entering the grounds, the dazzling **Golden Temple Bell** catches your eye. Its sound is said to purify the mind and drive away negative energy. In the magnificent main hall, the **'Goma (Homa) prayer'** is held several times a day. The ritual of burning wooden amulets in the roaring flames to dispel misfortune is a cathartic experience just to watch.

#### **Reisen (Sacred Spring) and Zeniarai (Money Washing)**

Within the precincts, there is a **'Reisen'** (sacred spring) where clear water gushes forth. It is said that washing money in the flowing water in front of the 'Zeniarai Benten' statue here will increase your financial luck. Keep the washed money in your wallet as 'seed money'!

#### **Statue of Tsunodaishi**

He may look frightening, but he's actually a reliable guardian deity! Look for the statue of **'Tsunodaishi'**. His skeletal ribs and horned appearance are said to make all evil spirits flee in terror.
